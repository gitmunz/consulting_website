The real bottleneck: integration

Across industries, leading AI vendors are increasingly deploying forward-deployed engineers to embed directly with customers. Why? Because off-the-shelf agents rarely deliver value out of the box. Impact emerges when agents are deeply aligned with an organization’s unique logic and context.

Biology doesn’t fit generic agents

In bio sciences, the challenge is amplified. Research environments are characterized by extreme heterogeneity: bespoke pipelines, legacy tools, evolving standards and fragmented data formats.

Integration May Require

  • Working natively with existing data models
  • Coordinating external tools and cloud infrastructure
  • Ensuring reproducibility, auditability, data security
  • Supporting human-in-the-loop review and oversight

This level of integration goes beyond smart prompting. It requires hands-on system design and engineering taylored to your use case.
